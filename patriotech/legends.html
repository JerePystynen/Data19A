The Legends Of The League is a free Sci-fi RTS game that happens in future where there are all sorts of aliens and mysterious creatures to fight with and against.

In Legends Of The League ("Legends", for short), you can play in three different gamemodes and play as orgs, humans or aliens. Aliens are fast moving creatures. Orgs are like human but with more muscle. They have worse breath though.

3 different gamemodes. Play on both PC and mobile.
